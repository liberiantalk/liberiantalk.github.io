Welcome to Liberian Talk

Liberian Talk is a thought provoking Social, Political and Economic platform which aims to promote real peace and democracy in Liberia. With emphasis on enlightenment, education, information, and current events, the program seeks to provide a platform for each and every Liberian citizen to have a say in the policies and decisions that affects their day to day activities.

We play Host to everyone on everything; daring to boldly go where many dare not go; to inform and educate our esteemed Liberian citizens on national and international issues. We bring you modern contemporary politics, news, music and entertainment in addition to sports and religion.
